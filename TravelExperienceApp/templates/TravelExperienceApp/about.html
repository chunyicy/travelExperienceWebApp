ABOUT US

The Agile Travelogue by Team 38
Our team has decided to create a travel experience website, our travel experience website is a platform that allows users to simply start on creating a post to share their travel experience stories. Our platform is more towards photo and content sharing social networking. It is also capable of providing updated travel information based on user’s input and enabling better travel decisions. Through the use of our travel experience website platform, users are able to search posts, create post, commenting on a post, like & share a post, edit post, edit profile etc.

We believe that this idea could be developed further and better integrated to bring about greater convenience to the users by providing a web application for the people’s needs in sharing their meaningful journey to another country.
